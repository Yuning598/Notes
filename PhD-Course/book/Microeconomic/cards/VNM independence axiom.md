# VNM independence axiom

Main note: [Microeconomic/06_Equilibrium_Under_Uncertainty](../Microeconomic/06_Equilibrium_Under_Uncertainty)

Independence:

$$
L\succeq L'
\Longleftrightarrow
\alpha L+(1-\alpha)L''\succeq \alpha L'+(1-\alpha)L''
$$

for \(\alpha\in(0,1)\).

Economic meaning: common lottery branches cancel. This is what makes indifference curves in the probability simplex straight and parallel, and it is what makes utility linear in probabilities.

# Risk aversion and Jensen

Main note: [Microeconomic/06_Equilibrium_Under_Uncertainty](../Microeconomic/06_Equilibrium_Under_Uncertainty)

Risk aversion:

$$
u(E[g])\ge E[u(g)].
$$

Concavity implies risk aversion by Jensen. Strict concavity implies strict dislike of non-degenerate mean-preserving risk.

Certainty equivalent:

$$
u(CE)=E[u(g)].
$$

Risk premium:

$$
CE=E[g]-\pi.
$$

【句子含义】
- As a result of crustal adjustments and faulting, the Strait of Gibraltar, where the Mediterranean now connects to the Atlantic, opened, and water cascaded spectacularly back into the Mediterranean.
> <span style="font-family:.PingFangUITextSC-Regular;">由于地壳调整和断层作用，现在地中海与大西洋相连的直布罗陀海峡张开，海水壮观地流回地中海。</span>
> - <span style="font-family:.PingFangUITextSC-Regular;">crustal adjustment + faulting -> strait open -> water back in to Mediterranean 地壳调整和断层作用，导致G海峡张开，大西洋海水壮观回流地中海（也就是两个海洋相连）。</span>
> - <span style="font-family:.PingFangUITextSC-Regular;">[S reopen -> cascade -> crustal adjustment and faulting，因果错误，是地壳活动导致海峡张开、海水回流，不是海峡张开、海水回流导致了地壳活动]</span> A. The Strait of Gibraltar reopened \[when the Mediterranean and the Atlantic became connected\] <b><u>and</u></b> the cascades of water from one sea to the other <b><u>caused</u></b> crustal adjustments and faulting.
> - \[<u>crustal adjustments and faulting -> G strait open -> M refill</u>，正确\] B. The Mediterranean was dramatically <b><u>refilled</u></b> by water from the Atlantic <b><u>when</u></b> crustal adjustments and faulting opened the Strait of Gibraltar, the place where the two seas are joined.
> - \[明显错误，没有提到瀑布不如地壳断层壮观\] C. The cascades of water from the Atlantic to the Mediterranean <u>were not as spectacular</u> as the crustal adjustments and faulting that occurred when the Strait of Gibraltar was connected to those seas.
> - \[并不是连成一片海，而是两个海洋相连\] D. As a result of crustal adjustments and faulting and the <u>creation of the Strait of Gibraltar</u>, the Atlantic and Mediterranean were connected and <u>became a single sea</u> with spectacular cascades of water between them.

- Like the stones of a Roman wall, which <u>were held together</u> both by the regularity of the design and by that peculiarly powerful Roman cement, so the various parts of the Roman realm <span style="font-size:12.333333015441895pt;"><u>were bonded into</u></span> a massive, monolithic entity by physical, organizational, and psychological controls.
> <span style="font-family:.PingFangUITextSC-Regular;">就像罗马城墙的石头一样，它们通过设计的规则性和那种特别强大的罗马水泥固定在一起，罗马王国的各个部分也通过物理、组织和心理控制粘合成一个巨大的、整体的实体。</span>
> - <span style="font-family:.PingFangUITextSC-Regular;">罗马王国的各部分是一个巨大的整体，和城墙的石头一样（设计的规则性+水泥 = 物理、组织和心理控制粘合）</span>
> - <span style="font-family:.PingFangUITextSC-Regular;">抓住关键词，like were held together = were bonded into，所以句子本质上是在谈整体性像设计和水泥一样，而非用城墙控制王国或者用制度维护领土。</span>

【排除题】
- Whereas Greece had grown from <u>scores of</u> scattered cities, Rome grew from one single organism. While the Greek world had expanded along the Mediterranean sea lanes, the Roman world was assembled by territorial conquest.
> - 希腊是从数十个分散的城市发展而来的，罗马是从单一的有机体发展而来的（expansion from one original settlement）
> - 尽管Greek是沿着地中海航道扩张的（expansion by sea invasion），但罗马世界是通过领地征服形成的（territorial expansion）。
